Surrender

NOTE: You cannot surrender items online, you must be present at the hub to surrender an item.

The surrendered items in the Lost and Found AI-powered hub can be rest assured to reach their true owner through several methods. The AI-powered hub uses advanced NLP text analyzation algorithms technology to match lost items with their description in the database.

Claim


The process of claiming an item in the Lost and Found AI hub involves several steps to ensure the item is returned to the rightful owner. The claimant must provide their claiming code, which they would have received when searching for an item and matched the description process.

The AI-powered hub uses advanced text matching technology to match the item with its description in the database, and the claimant may also be required to take a picture of themselves with the item for added protection. Once the AI confirms the item belongs to the claimant, it will be released to them. This process ensures that only the rightful owner can claim the item, and provides added security against fraud or theft.
